Dhaki union

I live at Dhaki, a small village in Krishrogonj district. The population of the village is around one thousand. Most of them are farmers, few are small traders. Our village has one primary school, a sub-post office and a primary health center. Besides, there are a police outpost and a block office which serve greatly to the needs of the villagers. The village has a few kutcha roads, tanks and playgrounds. In our village, we all lead a happy social life with occasional pujas, jatras and melas. The gram panchayat looks after our welfare. The villagers, though faced with many disadvantages, are on the whole happy and contented.
